Arabella Sinclair

Assistant Professor, University of Aberdeen

I am a Lecturer/Assistant professor at the Universtiy of Aberdeen. My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Psycholingusitcs and Cognitive Science. My favourite projects involve interdisciplinary challenges, using theories of human interaction or learning to address the challenge of applying language technologies to the fields of dialogoue modelling, education and human-centred interaction.

Giulia Pucci

PhD Student, University of Aberdeen

Giulia is a first year Phd Student, co-supervised with Ehud Reiter. With a strong background in Linguistics, she is exploring adaptive, audience-aware generation in Langauge Models.

Irina Guliaeva

Phd Student, University of Aberdeen

Irina is a first year Phd Student in the School of Psychology, working on a competitive EastBio project that studies links between Structural Priming in Humans and Language Models. Co-supervised by Anastasia Klimovich Gray, Arabella Sinclair and Agnieszka Konopka.

Jin Luo

PhD Student, University of Aberdeen

Jin is a first year PhD student in the School of School of Language, Literature, Music and Visual Culture. He is researching LMs for EFL learner essay feedback, exploring their use both as an aide for tutors, as well as a tool for learners. Jin is supervised by Elspeth Edelstein, co-supervised by Arabella.
