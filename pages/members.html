Arabella Sinclair

Assistant Professor, University of Aberdeen

I am a Lecturer/Assistant professor at the Universtiy of Aberdeen. My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Psycholingusitcs and Cognitive Science. My favourite projects involve interdisciplinary challenges, using theories of human interaction or learning to address the challenge of applying language technologies to the fields of dialogoue modelling, education and human-centred interaction.

Former Members

Some students of the lab stay on to collaborate on extensions to their thesis projects that result in publications.

Jun Sen Yee

KTP Associate,

Joe did his MSc AI at the University of Aberdeen, thesis supervised by Arabella. He then went on to extend some of the work done in his thesis and collaborate with us on Efficiency and Effectiveness in Task-Oriented Dialogue: On Construction Repetition, Information Rate, and Task Success. Aron Molnar

Research Assistant

Aron did his BSc Honours in Computing Science at the Universtiy of Aberdeen, with his thesis supervised by Arabella. He then went on to extend some of the work done in his thesis and collaborate with us on Attribution and Alignment: Effects of Local Context Repetition on Utterance Production and Comprehension in Dialogue. He then joined the lab as a research assistant. He is now working as an LLM engineer in Cambridge, but continues to collaborate with us.

Student Supervision
